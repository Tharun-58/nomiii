FAQ

How do I reset my password?

Go to Settings > Security > Change Password. Enter your current password and set a new one.

What should I do if a delivery is delayed?

Contact the customer immediately to inform them. You can also notify support for assistance with traffic or route issues.

How are performance metrics calculated?

Metrics are calculated based on actual delivery times compared to scheduled times, customer feedback, and successful deliveries.

Can I change my delivery zone?

Yes, you can change your preferred zone in Settings > App Preferences > Default Zone.
